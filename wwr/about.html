Our Story

Founded in 1995 by adventure enthusiasts Marc and Sarah Thompson, Akwé Whitewater Rafting began as a small family operation with just two rafts and a dream to share the beauty of Congo's pristine waterways. Named "Akwé" after the local word for "water spirit," our company has grown into the region's premier rafting outfitter while maintaining our commitment to environmental stewardship and authentic experiences.

Over nearly three decades, we've guided thousands of adventurers through some of the most spectacular rapids in Central Africa. Our team of certified guides brings together decades of experience, local knowledge, and an unwavering passion for both safety and excitement. We've weathered economic challenges, expanded our fleet to include specialized equipment for all skill levels, and built lasting partnerships with local communities.

Today, Akwé Whitewater Rafting stands as a testament to the power of pursuing your passion while respecting the natural world that makes our adventures possible. Every trip we offer reflects our core values: safety first, environmental responsibility, and creating transformative experiences that connect people with the raw beauty of untamed rivers.
